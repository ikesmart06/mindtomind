Who We Are

Mind To Mind Initiative is a non-profit, nongovernmental organization set up with the primary purpose of changing and empowering the lives of young people. We believe that for any society to be transformed, there must first be a transformation in the minds of its citizens, hence our motto is “Change begins in the mind”

Our Mission & Vision

At Mind to Mind Initiative Africa, we are guided by a clear mission and a bold vision for change.

Our Mission

To create platforms where future leaders can be inspired, motivated and mentored for greatness

Our Vision

To raise leaders who, through character, integrity, ingenuity,and a no-excuse spirit, will impact their world positively and leave behind good legacies
